The Next Generation in SpeedRunning.

Meet the new, redesigned Speed Demos Archives. Now with 86% more Kreygasmic features !

A whole new look !

"New WR for Web Design HTML5% !"

Speed Demos Archives’ visual aspect has never changed since it‘s creation in 1999. With the new trends in website design, we figured it was time.


With the help of a professionnal web developer, we’ve redesigned the entire site in order to make it more attractive to the eye. You’ll find that it’s way more enjoyable to browse, unless you hate change, that is !

Going social !

“RunnerGuy2489, SimpleFlips and 9 others like this.”

Facebook, Twitter... these are ways you can get to know more about popular speedrunners and what they are doing. Sadly, not everyone chooses to use these social networks.


Thankfully, every user on Speed Demos Archives is part of our social network division, which means you can get updates from every speedrunner out there, no matter how anti-social they are ! Share, like, comment... You can now do it all on a single website !

Notifications get you notified !

“Hey, listen ! Skater82297 got OoT any% World Record !”

It’s very easy to miss a world record, a live stream or a new discovered strats for certains games. There is no way to have this on a single screen!


Speed Demos Archives solves this with an alert system that’s integrated to the system and in the social features. Hop into instant email alerts to get these notifications easily from a single source, or find them directly on your social dashboard !

Enjoyable for newbies !

“Don’t worry, you’ll get that sub 20 if you persevere !”

For the average gamer, getting into speedrunning can seem scary. “There’s people way better than me already who have years of experience, how can I enjoy speedrunning if I’m not as good at the best ?” is what might be on these people’s minds.


Fortunately, we took the time to make speedrunning as easy, fun and satisfying to get into as possible. All members have access to a large array of resources on how to speedrun specific games, and we provide a journal system that lets you keep track of your progress as a speedrunner. It’s like a weight-watcher, except with timers and video games !

Customized Runs !

“The glossy effect makes teh urn possible.”

We want to make sure that our users get a customized experience, whether they’re browsing for speedruns or posting their own. That is why we came up with the idea of customizing the look of a run’s icon as it will appear on the game list, on search results and on user profiles.


Now, if you’re the owner of a speedrun that’s in the top 3 of any category of any game, you can change it’s visual style that’ll be shown on the demo list ! This ensures that the top speedruns will get the most attention! If you have SDA Premium though, all of your published runs can have this, no matter how good they are!

Twitch & LiveSplits Integration !

“My runs are published before they’ve even started* !”

SpeedRunners have adopted Twitch.tv since it’s inception for their streaming shenanigans. Meanwhile, since the creation of LiveSplits, it has become the #1 split program for speedrunning. What if those two worked together with your SDA account ?


That’s right, Speed Demos Archives can publish a new status update everytime you are live, so that everyone may know about it ! Meanwhile, LiveSplits will take care of syncing your splits with the timer on your streaming page, so that you will not need to put them in your stream capture area anymore ! Talk about convenience !

(*It doesn’t ACTUALLY do that. C’mon, don’t be silly !)
